# Robbinsville Premier League - Team Owner Guide

Welcome to the RPL Live Auction! This guide explains how to log in, navigate the bidding interface, and manage your squad budget.

## 1. Logging In
1. Navigate to the URL provided by your League Admin.
2. Select your Team Name from the dropdown menu.
3. Enter the password provided to you by the Admin.
4. *Do not share your password.* If someone else logs into your team, they will be able to spend your budget!

## 2. The Bidding Interface
Once logged in, you will be on the main **Live Auction** screen.

*   **Player Card (Left):** Displays the current player up for auction, their designated Pool, and their Base Price.
*   **Current Bid (Center):** Displays the highest bid and the name of the team currently winning.
*   **Bid Button:** Click the large blue button to place a bid. The button will always display the *minimum* amount required to take the lead (e.g., "Bid X pts").
*   **Timer:** Keep an eye on the countdown ring. When you place a bid, the timer is usually bumped back up slightly (e.g., +10 seconds) to give others a chance to respond.

## 3. Managing Your Budget & Roster
Your team has strict roster and budget constraints enforced by the server. 

### The Budget Formula
You have a maximum `Squad Size` (e.g., 18 players) and a globally defined `Minimum Bid` (e.g., 1,000 pts). 
You **must** keep enough points in reserve to physically fill out your entire roster. 

*   *Example:* If you have 3 open slots remaining, and the minimum bid is 1,000 pts, the app will permanently lock away 2,000 pts from your visible budget to ensure you can afford two 1,000-pt players later. You are only allowed to bid up to your "Max Allowable Bid".
*   If your **Bid Button is disabled and grayed out**, it means placing that bid would mathematically prevent you from filling your squad requirements later.

### Viewing Your Progress
*   **Top Nav Bar:** Your remaining Budget and current Squad Size (e.g., `12 / 18`) are pinned to the top of your screen at all times.
*   **Roster Sidebar:** Click the "My Roster" button in the navigation bar to slide out a panel showing every player you have successfully purchased. The players are sorted automatically by their **Average Points** so you can easily identify your highest-value players.

## 4. Tips for the Auction
*   **Speed matters:** If the timer is blinking red, get your bid in. Depending on the Admin's settings, the player might be sold automatically the absolute millisecond the timer hits zero.
*   **Don't panic on mistakes:** If you accidentally place a bid, or the system registers a bid you didn't mean to make, shout out to the Admin immediately! The Admin has a "Pause" button and the ability to manually override sale prices or return a player to the auction block. 
*   **Watch the Dashboard:** If you have a second monitor, open the `/dashboard` read-only link. It shows a grid of every opposing team's remaining budget and roster size, so you know exactly who you are bidding against and how much money they have left!
