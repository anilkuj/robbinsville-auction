Phase 3

Budget & Squad Rules

The app enforces mathematical fairness. You can never "overshoot" your budget.

The Reserve Rule

You must keep enough points to pay the Minimum Bid for all remaining squad slots.

Budget Logic
Remaining Slots: 5
Min Bid: 1,000 pts
Reserved: 4,000 pts (for slots 2-5)
Max Allowable Bid: Budget - 4,000

Squad Builder

Use the sidebar to track your roster. It displays:

✔ Every player purchased
✔ Cost of each player
✔ Total points spent
⚠️ If your Bid button is grayed out, it means you don't have enough budget left while still obeying the Reserve Rule.

Auction Tips

📞

Shout Errors

Keep track of how many players are left in each pool to gauge scarcity.

Jump Bids

Use custom bid amounts to intimidate rivals on high-value players.
